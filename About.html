About Global Keen Solutions

Global Keen Solutions promoted & managed by professionals, is in the business of financial intermediation, consultancy, IT staffing, out sourcing and many domains. We also render our services in respect of head hunting & manpower planning and help you get the right candidate for your enterprise. Mr. Siva Kumar & Mr. Sreenivas, Global Keen Solution’s founders and Directors, believed that the IT infrastructure of the future would be managed not by people, but by expert systems. He envisioned a world in which the mundane engineering chores would be automated, freeing engineers and technologists to focus on creative endeavours and innovation. So Siva Kumar & Sreenivas founded Global Keen Solutions based on this principle. Within a few years of its founding, Global Keen Solutions established a presence in India. As growth continued to accelerate, new offices followed. First in Hyderabad In 2009 alone, Global Keen opened its Cheenai and Banglore offices. The pace of innovation matched the pace of growth, as Global Keen Solutions’ engineers developed the company’s unique technologies. Each new innovation built upon the last, maintaining a continual state of evolution. In little more than a decade, Global Keen Solutions has evolved from small start up to emerging giant. Global Keen Solutions now has ten offices on three continents. Many Companies are Global Keen clients. Global Keen solution’s founding dream is now reality: About 90 percent of all client problems are resolved without human intervention. What defines Global Keen Solutions is simple: the constaive to do, and to be, better.
